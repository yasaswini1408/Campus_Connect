Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about using Campus Connect.

Account & Access

Use your official University ID (e.g., U123456) and your standard campus network password. If you are having trouble, please use the 'Forgot Password' link, which will direct you to the central university login portal.

Campus Connect uses your university's central authentication system. You must reset your password via the official university IT support page linked on our login screen. We do not store or manage your credentials directly.

Academic Features

Attendance is updated daily by your instructor's system. Exam Results and official grades are updated within 24 hours of being posted by the Registrar's Office or faculty, following institutional approval processes.

Your timetable is synced directly with the university scheduling system. If the schedule has not been officially released yet, it will not appear. Please check the Academic Calendar for the official release date.

Navigate to the 'My Courses' section, select the relevant course, and look under the 'Materials' tab. All lecture notes, documents, and external links shared by your faculty are organized there.

Community & Support

Discussion Groups allow students to connect for study, projects, and general interests. While they are student-driven, all groups are monitored for adherence to the Student Code of Conduct. You can report inappropriate behavior using the 'Report' function within any group.

The 'Ask a Doubt' feature directs your question to the relevant course community. It is primarily answered by verified peers and, occasionally, teaching assistants or faculty. You will receive a notification when a solution is posted.

Can't find your answer? Contact our Support Team.
